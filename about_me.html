About Me

Introduction

Hello! I am a physicist specializing in condensed matter physics. I have a PhD in superconductivity and quantum materials research, and I currently work on projects related to superconductors, 2D material devices, and their physics. I also teach undergraduate and graduate courses in superconductivity, solid state physics, and basic physics.

Research

My research focuses on the exploration of new superconductors, studying materials under extreme conditions, and understanding quantum phenomena in novel materials. I am particularly interested in high-pressure physics, and I have experience with Raman spectroscopy and DAC systems for simulating Earth's internal environments.

Other Interests

In addition to my research, I am passionate about science outreach, especially for younger audiences. I enjoy demonstrating experiments like magnetic levitation using superconductors to inspire interest in physics.
